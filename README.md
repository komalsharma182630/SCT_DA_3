# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze employee turnover and identify the major reasons behind attrition within an organization.

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Identify departments with the highest attrition.
* Examine attrition across different age groups.
* Evaluate the impact of overtime on employee turnover.
* Discover key factors influencing employee exits.
* Provide interactive filtering for better decision-making.

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Workforce Size (Total Employees)
* Employee Exits
* Attrition Rate

### Interactive Filters

* Department Filter
* Age Group Analysis

### Visualizations

* Attrition by Department (Donut Chart)
* Attrition by Age Group (Bar Chart)
* Overtime Drives Attrition (Pie/Column Chart)
* Key Influencers Analysis
* Key Findings Summary

## Key Insights

* Employees aged 26–35 show the highest attrition.
* Overtime significantly increases employee turnover.
* Research & Development experiences the highest attrition.
* Younger employees are more likely to leave the organization.
* Multiple factors such as age, overtime, and job satisfaction influence employee attrition.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* HR Employee Attrition Dataset

## Business Impact

The dashboard helps HR teams identify high-risk employee segments, understand turnover drivers, and develop retention strategies to reduce attrition and improve workforce stability.

## Conclusion

This dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of employee attrition patterns. By leveraging data visualization and predictive insights, organizations can make informed HR decisions and improve employee retention.
